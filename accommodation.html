About This Accommodation

Welcome to Casa Rosa, a lovingly restored 18th-century family home in the heart of Lisbon's historic Alfama district. Our guesthouse offers a genuine Portuguese experience, where you'll stay in a traditional building while enjoying modern comforts.

The building features original azulejo tiles, wooden beams, and traditional architecture, while each room has been thoughtfully updated with comfortable beds, modern bathrooms, and amenities for today's traveler. From our rooftop terrace, you'll enjoy breathtaking views of the Tagus River and the terracotta rooftops of Alfama.

The guesthouse is run by the Rosa family, who have lived in this neighborhood for generations. Maria and João are your hosts, ready to share their insider knowledge of Lisbon's hidden gems, from the best small fado houses to authentic local restaurants tourists rarely discover.

Your Hosts

Maria and João Rosa are lifelong Alfama residents who converted their family home into a guesthouse in 2005. They live on the ground floor and are always available to help guests with recommendations, directions, or just a friendly conversation over a glass of Portuguese wine.

"We love sharing our neighborhood with visitors who want to experience the real Lisbon. Our guests often arrive as tourists but leave as friends."

Location

Casa Rosa is located in the heart of Alfama, Lisbon's oldest neighborhood. This charming, labyrinthine district is known for its narrow streets, historic buildings, and authentic fado music venues.

  • 5-minute walk to São Jorge Castle
  • 350m from Portas do Sol viewpoint
  • 500m from Lisbon Cathedral
  • Tram 28 stop (200m away)
